order-To make a delicious cup of coffee, you'll need filtered water, coffee grounds, and a coffee maker. Start by adding filtered water to the coffee maker's reservoir, using about 6 ounces of water per serving. Place a paper filter in the holder, then add 1 tablespoon of coffee grounds for each serving into the filter. If you prefer stronger coffee, use 2 tablespoons instead. Turn on the coffee maker and wait for it to finish brewing, which usually takes about 5 minutes. Once done, turn off the coffee maker, remove the filter, and serve the coffee. You can enjoy it black or add milk, creamer, or sweetener to taste¹².
To make a perfect espresso at home, start by selecting high-quality coffee beans and grinding them to a fine consistency. Fill the water reservoir of your espresso machine with fresh, cold water and turn it on to heat up. While it’s heating, place the ground coffee into the portafilter and tamp it down firmly to ensure an even extraction.
